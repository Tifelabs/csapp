\section*{Solution}

Using Amdahl's Law:

\begin{equation*}
    4 = \frac{1}{(1 - 0.9) + \frac{0.9}{k}}
\end{equation*}

\begin{align*}
    4 &= \frac{1}{0.1 + 0.9/k} \\
    0.4 + \frac{3.6}{k} &= 1.0 \\
    \frac{3.6}{k} &= 0.6 \\
    k &= 6.0
\end{align*}
