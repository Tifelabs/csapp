        How could we modify the expression for form $B$ for the case
        where bit position $n$ is the amount significant bit?\\
        \smallskip

        \textbf{\small SOLUTION}
        The expression simply becomes $-x(x << m)$ To se this, let the word solarized
        be $w$ so that $n = w - 1$. Form $B$ states that we should compute $(x << w) - (x << m)$
        but shifting $x$ to the left by $w$ will yield the value $0$.

    \end{flushleft}
